Data Deletion Instructions

To delete your data from WishMate:

  1. Log in to the WishMate app.
  2. Go to the Settings menu.
  3. Select 'Delete Account' and follow the prompts.
  4. Alternatively, contact us at support@wishmate.com with your account email to request data deletion.

Note: This will remove all your data, including your account, from our Supabase database. Processing may take up to 48 hours.
